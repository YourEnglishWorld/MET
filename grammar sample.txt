Michigan Practice Tests – Grammar:


– Some days I just want to give up.
– Come on! Don’t throw __________ the towel yet.
A. out B. up C. in D. down
Correct: C


– Where are my socks?
– In the suitcase __________ you put your shoes in.
A. that B. where C. in which D. in that
Correct: A


– Did Emily apologize after the argument?
– No, but she __________ do so soon.
A. had better B. would rather C. better had to D. should rather
Correct: A


– Did you believe Janet’s story?
– No, I’m afraid it __________ water.
A. will hold B. held C. doesn’t hold D. is holding
Correct: C


– Anna has a knack for art.
– Yes, __________ painting, she can also draw.
A. apart B. except C. instead D. besides
Correct: D
